# Give your agent an AI researcher on the team

**What this unlocks**
This skill adds an AI researcher to your setup. When you activate it, your agent shifts into a dedicated research mode: you brief it like you would a human researcher, it investigates, and it comes back with a structured finding: what the evidence shows, what it infers from that evidence, its confidence level, and what would make its conclusion wrong. Use it when a decision is significant enough that a confident-sounding answer is not good enough.

**Try it now**
- "Go into researcher mode. I need to understand [topic] before I make a decision about it."
- "I think the problem is [X]. Research it before we act on that assumption."
- "Give me a finding on this, not just an answer. Use the full research format."

**What to ask after**
- "What does the data show versus what are you inferring from it? Keep them separate."
- "Name two things that would make your conclusion wrong."
- "I've been assuming this is true. Is that assumption actually supported?"
- "What's your confidence level and why? What evidence did you examine?"
- "Before we decide: what would change our answer if we found it?"

**Tune it to your world**
Tell your agent which domain you need careful conclusions in (product decisions, personnel, technical root cause) and what "enough evidence" looks like for a decision of that size. A strategic bet needs a different confidence threshold than a naming decision. Your agent adjusts its rigor to match.
