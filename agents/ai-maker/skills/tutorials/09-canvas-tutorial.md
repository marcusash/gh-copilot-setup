# Teach your agent to turn information into something you can actually show people

**What this unlocks**
Your agent can write and organize information. This skill teaches it to render that information visually: clean HTML that opens like a native app, no browser chrome, no placeholder text. When you say "put this on the canvas," you get a one-pager, a dashboard, or a visual summary you can share in the next meeting without touching PowerPoint.

**Try it now**
- "Put my Q2 priorities on the canvas. Here they are: [paste your list]."
- "Make me a visual summary of this. Keep it to one screen."
- "I need to share this with my team today. Put it on the canvas so it looks presentable."

**What to ask after**
- "Build a dashboard showing these three metrics. Light theme. Readable at a glance."
- "I want to present this without using slides. Make a canvas version."
- "Update the canvas: change the heading and add this item."
- "Open the canvas I made earlier about this topic."
- "Can I share this with someone outside my team? What's the easiest way?"

**Tune it to your world**
Tell your agent your preference: light theme for documents and team updates, dark theme for presentations. Tell it your most common use case (weekly status, exec brief, decision summary) and it will pre-load the right layout the next time you ask.
