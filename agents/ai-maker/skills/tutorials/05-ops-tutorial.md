# Teach your agent to keep work moving without being told what to do next

**What this unlocks**
Your agent stops waiting for you to direct every step. After this skill, it tracks priorities, knows what needs a human decision versus what it can handle, routes requests to the right person, and writes messages that lead with the ask instead of burying it. When you hand it a task, it finishes and tells you what comes next before you have to ask.

**Try it now**
- "Here are 5 things I need to get done this week. Help me prioritize."
- "I need to send a message asking for a decision on this. Write it so the ask is in the first sentence."
- "What should I work on right now based on what we've discussed today?"

**What to ask after**
- "I just finished this task. What's next?"
- "This is blocked. What do I need to unblock it and who do I need it from?"
- "Draft a message that gets a decision by end of day. Here's the context."
- "Review my week. What's not moving that should be?"
- "I have a decision I keep putting off. Help me frame it and route it to the right person."

**Tune it to your world**
Tell your agent your priority system (P1/P2/P3, urgent/important, whatever you use), how many things you realistically ship in a day, and who the key decision-makers are on your team. The more it knows about how you operate, the less you have to repeat yourself.
