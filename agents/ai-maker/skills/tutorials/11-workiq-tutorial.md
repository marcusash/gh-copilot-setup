# Teach your agent to answer work questions without asking you to summarize your own calendar

**What this unlocks**
Your agent can now read your Microsoft 365 data directly: email, calendar, Teams, documents. When you ask "what's on my plate today" or "what did we decide in the channel last week," it checks your actual data instead of asking you to recap it. You stop being the middleman between your tools and your assistant.

**Try it now**
- "What meetings do I have today and who is in each one?"
- "Find emails from [person] about [topic] from the last two weeks."
- "What decisions came out of my last staff meeting?"

**What to ask after**
- "Summarize the Teams conversation in [channel] about [topic]."
- "What documents has my team been working on this week?"
- "Who has been most active in [channel] lately?"
- "I missed a meeting yesterday. What do I need to know from it?"
- "Pull my sent mail from last week. What did I commit to that I haven't followed up on?"

**Tune it to your world**
WorkIQ authenticates once with your Microsoft account and then runs automatically. No repeated login. You can focus it by naming specific people, channels, or time windows. The more specific the question, the more specific the answer.
