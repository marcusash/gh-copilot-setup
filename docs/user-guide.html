What is AI Maker?

AI Maker is a personal AI partner that lives on your PC. You type what you need in plain English, just like a Teams message, and it helps you get things done. It remembers your work, can open visual dashboards in your browser, and connects to your Microsoft tools.

What AI Maker can do
🖼️

Canvas

Ask AI Maker to "open a canvas" and it builds a visual right here in your browser. Project dashboards, status reports, slide decks. No PowerPoint required.

🗄️

Vault

Your personal notes folder. Say "save this to my vault" and AI Maker writes it to a file on your PC, organized and always easy to find. Like OneDrive, but local.

📋

WorkIQ Tasks

Connected to Microsoft WorkIQ. Ask "what's on my task list?" or "add a task to follow up with design." Works alongside your existing M365 workflow.

🧠

Skills

AI Maker has 8 built-in skills: research, design, writing, code review, data analysis, brainstorming, user research, and communication. Just describe what you need.
